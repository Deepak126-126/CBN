About Chandrababu Naidu

Chandrababu Naidu is a prominent political figure with a rich history of public service. Born on April 20, 1950, in Naravaripalli, Andhra Pradesh, he has played a crucial role in shaping the political landscape of the region.

Chandrababu Naidu served as the Chief Minister of Andhra Pradesh multiple times and is known for his innovative policies and commitment to development.
